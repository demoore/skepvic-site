SkepVics Constitution

ARTICLE I: Name and Purpose

Section I

This organization will be known as "SkepVics".

Section II

The purpose of the organization shall be:

  1. To promote scientific inquiry and critical thinking
  2. To provide a safe and welcoming community for skeptics and those interested in learning more about skepticism as a movement.
  3. To organize events and activities that are of interest to skeptics.

ARTICLE II: Membership

Membership will be open to all University of Victoria students, faculty, staff, and community members with no less than two thirds of the members of a club shall be UVic students.


ARTICLE III: Governance

Section I

SkepVics will have an executive of no less than three; at least two of whom will be UVic students. The executive will be elected by the membership of the club once per year.Election shall occur by secret ballot at the Annual General Meeting, or at a meeting scheduled for the express purpose of election The candidates with the most votes win; uncontested candidate win by default. No members of the club shall be denied the right to vote in the election of the executive of the club.

Section II

If for any reason an officer is unable or unwilling to fulfill their commitments, they may resign and the remaining elected officers may appoint, via majority vote, replacement(s) who will serve until the next election period.
